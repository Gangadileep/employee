Employee Registration


Once the employer registration formalities are over, employer shall register all employees under the establishment with particulars such as Name, Father's Name, Address, Date of Birth, Gender, Date of Joining, Family Details, Nominee Details, and a declaration. Once registered, in respect of employees who are being registered under the scheme afresh, an insurance number is allotted and a Temporary Identity Card (TIC) is generated.
